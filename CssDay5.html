Software engineering is the systematic process of designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software applications to meet specific needs. It combines principles of computer science, engineering, and project management to ensure that software is reliable, efficient, and scalable. Software engineers use structured methods and tools to build programs that solve real-world problems, ranging from mobile apps to large-scale enterprise systems.

Programs must be written for people to read, and only incidentally for machines to execute.
