A bicycle, also called a cycle, is a two-wheeled vehicle that is powered by pedaling. It is one of the most eco-friendly and healthy modes of transportation. Cycles are used by people of all ages for traveling short distances, exercising, and even for sports like racing and mountain biking.

Modern bicycles come in different types such as road bikes, mountain bikes, and hybrid bikes. They are usually made of lightweight materials like aluminum or carbon fiber and have advanced gear systems to make riding easier on different terrains.
